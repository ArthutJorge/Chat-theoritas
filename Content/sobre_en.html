〖 About 〗

The Theoritas Chat is a voluntary project started on Cellbit's Discord with the aim of keeping members updated on events they might have missed. Over time, we have grown and gained official recognition to represent the community!

〖 Who we are? 〗

We are a team formed by members who enjoy QSMP. Each one has specific roles within our group, such as updating QSMP Diaries and managing our social media accounts. We maintain this initiative to support the community.

〖 Operation 〗

The Theoritas Chat team is organized by the three founding leaders of the project. Together, we coordinate and guide the internal structure and recruit new members interested in joining and committing to our activities!
